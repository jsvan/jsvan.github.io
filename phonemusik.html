ABOUT:
Cant remember your phone numbers? Music is 100% guaranteed to work. Each number is associated with its scale degree, where 1 is tonic, 5 is the fifth, 8 is the octave. I use a major scale (for now). 0's are somewhat of a wild card, and I use advanced AI decision making techniques to send it to either the upper or the lower octave. Its up to you to remember which are the 0's :)
